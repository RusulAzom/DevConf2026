Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend Dev Conference 2026?

Engineers, founders, product managers, and anyone building software. If you code, design, or ship products, you’ll get value from the talks and workshops.

Are workshops included in all tickets?

Standard tickets include 2 workshop sessions. Pro and Team tickets get unlimited workshop access. Check the pricing section for full details.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. Standard tickets get 30-day access to recordings. Pro and Team tickets get lifetime access to all session recordings.

What’s the refund policy?

Full refunds available until August 1, 2026. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person up to 7 days before the event.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! We offer 50% off Standard tickets for students with a valid .edu email. Contact us at team@devconf2026.com with proof of enrollment.

Where is the venue and are hotels included?

The conference is at the Downtown Tech Center. Hotels aren’t included, but we have discounted partner rates. You’ll get booking details after registration.
